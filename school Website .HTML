Overview

Blooming Greens High School is an upscale learning centre in the heart of Lagos, Nigeria with a unique blend of the Nigerian and British curricula. The school is divided into the Junior Secondary and Senior Secondary sections. At Key Stage 3 (years 7- 9) the students spend three years and take the Cambridge Checkpoint exams as their end-of-course.

Subjects of Blooming Greens High School

Blooming Greens High School offers a wide range of subjects across various departments, including English, Mathematics, Sciences, Languages, ICT, and Humanities. They also have a strong focus on creative and performance arts, as well as vocational and sports-related subjects. Here's a more detailed breakdown: Core Subjects: English: English Language and Literature in English. Mathematics: Mathematics and Further Mathematics. Sciences: Biology, Physics, and Chemistry. Languages: French, Yoruba, and Igbo. ICT & Technology: Information and Communication Technology (ICT), Computer Science, Data Processing, Technical Drawing, and Design Technology. Humanities: Geography. Civic Education. Social Studies. IRK & CRK (Islamic Religious Knowledge & Christian Religious Knowledge). Government. Nigerian History. European History. Creative & Performance Arts: Music, Visual Arts and Design, Drama, and Dance. Vocational & Other: Agricultural Science. Food & Nutrition. Home Economics. Garment Making. Business Studies. Economics. Accounting. Marketing. Physical and Health Education.
